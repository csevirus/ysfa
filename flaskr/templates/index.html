YUVA SPORTS FEDERATION OF INDIA

--ABOUT YSFI--

YSFI was built to eradicate the unethical practices and to promote the sports culture amongs youth. Conducting championships at block , district , state , national and international level which helps the youth to connect with the sports world . This will help in boosting the morale of the youth to participate in various international level competitions such as common wealth, Olympics and Asiad and win medals for the country.

Games we conduct:

Kabaddi, circular Kabaddi, Yoga, Athletic, Boxing, Badminton, Hockey, Volleyball, Handball, Football, Cricket, Basketball, Kho-kho, Karate, rugby, taekwondo, tug of war, weight lifting, cycling, archery, cross country race, drop ro-boll, kick boxing, net ball, power lifting, jump rope, wushu, table tennis, shooting, air rifle, pistil, wrestling etc.
